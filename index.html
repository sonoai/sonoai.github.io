About

We understand the beauty of wellness.

Sona AI is an innovative company using artificial intelligence to interpret ultrasound images. We build efficient algorithms that make reading ultrasound images an extremely easy task. At sona AI we believe in multiskilled approach to development challenges. We are a team from different backgrounds; Innocent and Phyllis are medical radiographers with an indepth understanding of the challenges that are faced by radiographers and medical professionals in interpreting medical images. Benjamin and Benjamin have a fiery involvement in artificial intelligence. With medical and engineering backgrounds they provide an unbiased approach to writing algorithms

Expertise

We are experts in broad range of fields.

Mission

To make low cost ultrasound accessible and easy to use globally with a focus on the world's most isolated and vulnerable communities .

Vision

Our team boasts of data scientist that have worked in fields of image processing and healthcare solutions. Our Data scientists are not only thought leaders on the continent but also celebrated in healthcare.

Values

integrity, social good.
